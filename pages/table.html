What a balanced total look likes...

Balanced Total
Bench Deadlift Squat
300 500 400

As you can see here this lifter would max out at a 300 pound bench, 400 pound squat and 500 pound deadlift. This would equal a very nice 1200 total and mean that the lifter was very well balanced. While some people will be naturally stronger in some areas, it is important to figure out what your weaknesses are so you can focus on them more.
